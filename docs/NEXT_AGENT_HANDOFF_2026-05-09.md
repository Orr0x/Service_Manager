## What the seed script does
- Targets tenant:
  - Provided slug arg, or
  - `SEED_TENANT_SLUG`, or
  - newest `pw-smoke-company-*` tenant fallback.
- Adds:
  - `10` internal workers (+ auth users)
  - `10` external contractor users + contractor records
  - `10` customers
  - `50` job sites with **real nearby addresses** from Mapbox reverse geocoding around Burton on Trent area
  - Contracts across customers/sites (`1-3` per customer)
  - Checklist templates (ensures a set exists)
  - Scheduled jobs for next **6 weeks** (`Mon-Fri`, `5/day` = `150` jobs)
  - Job assignments mixed between workers and contractors
  - Job checklists attached per job (`1-3` per job)
- Generates credentials document for workbook-seeded users.
