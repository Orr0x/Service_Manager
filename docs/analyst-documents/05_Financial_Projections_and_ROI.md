## Executive Summary

The Service Mesh B2B SaaS platform presents a compelling financial opportunity with projected revenue growth from $60K in Year 1 to $10.8M by Year 3. The business model demonstrates strong unit economics with 85% gross margins and customer LTV:CAC ratio of 4.2:1.

Initial investment requirements of $750K are justified by the platform's unique market position and network effects, with projected break-even in Q4 Year 2 and positive cash flow from Q1 Year 3. The business shows strong scalability potential with operating leverage driving margin expansion as the business grows.

## 4. Unit Economics Analysis

### 4.1 Customer Lifetime Value (LTV)

**Assumptions:**
- Average Monthly Revenue: $150 ($1,800/year)
- Gross Margin: 77%
- Monthly Churn Rate: 3% (Annual 36% initially, declining to 15% by Year 3)

**LTV Calculation:**
- LTV = (Average Revenue × Gross Margin) / Monthly Churn Rate
- Year 1: ($150 × 77%) / 3% = $3,850
- Year 3: ($200 × 77%) / 1.25% = $12,320

### 4.2 Customer Acquisition Cost (CAC)

**Blended CAC by Year:**
- Year 1: $1,600
- Year 2: $1,400
- Year 3: $1,200

### 4.3 LTV:CAC Ratio

**Unit Economics Health:**
- Year 1: 2.4:1 (Below ideal, acceptable for early stage)
- Year 2: 2.8:1 (Improving, approaching healthy levels)
- Year 3: 10.3:1 (Excellent, strong profitability)

**CAC Payback Period:**
- Year 1: 10.7 months
- Year 2: 9.3 months
- Year 3: 6.0 months

## 9. Break-Even Analysis

### 9.1 Monthly Break-Even Calculation

**Fixed Monthly Costs:**
- Salaries: $100,000
- Rent & Utilities: $15,000
- Software & Tools: $10,000
- **Total Fixed Costs: $125,000**

**Variable Costs:**
- COGS: 23% of revenue
- Sales Commissions: 10% of revenue

**Break-Even Formula:**
- Fixed Costs ÷ (1 - Variable Cost %)
- $125,000 ÷ (1 - 33%) = $186,567 monthly revenue
- **Break-Even Customers: 124** (at $1,500/month average)

### 9.2 Time to Break-Even
